Speak the Words: Overview 

 

"Speak The Words" uses speech recognition software that allows learners to verbally respond to a question in ANVILL. The learners' answer has to match whatever you, the instructor, decide is the correct answer.

You can either create and embed a single Speak the Words question in your lesson, 

or use a "Speak the Words Set" to create multiple questions as one activity. Navigate here for an example of a Speak the Words Set based activity.
 

Important: Browser Compatibility

  • "Speak the Words" activities are only Google Chrome compatible.
  • You can download and install Google Chrome for free on Windows, Mac, or Linux

1. Access "Speak The Words" in Lesson

You have two options. To create a "Speak The Words" or "Speak The Words Set" activity. The first, "Speak the Words," allows you to create one question prompt. The second, "Speak The Words Set," allows you to create more than one prompt. 

  1. Login to ANVILL
  2. To add/edit Dictation a "Speak The Words" task in an existing lesson, navigate to your lesson page. Otherwise, create a new lesson.
  3. Once inside your lesson page:
  • Add New Speak the Words Activity
    Click on "+ Add Content."
    Select "Speak the Words" (or "Speak the Words Set") from the content menu.
  • Edit Existing Speak the Words Activity
    Click on the green pencil above your Speak the Words activity
    Select "Edit" from the dropdown menu.

2. Create "Speak The Words" Content

"Speak The Words" Tool (single prompt)

  1. Open the 'Speak the Words' edit screen. See Section 1.1 above to learn how.
  2. Give your' activity a title by typing over "Untitled Speak the Words"
  3. In the "Describe Task" box, write your question or prompt. 
    Ex. "What is Bart Simpson's father's name?"
  4. You can include one or more correct answers. Write the accepted answer or answers. To add more than one answer. just click "Add Item". 
    Ex. Homer Simpson
    Ex. Homer Jay Simpson
    Ex. Homer
  5. To save your work and exit, click on the check mark icon above your Speak the Words activity

"Speak the Words Set" Tool (multiple prompts)

  1. Open the 'Speak the Words Set' edit screen. See Section 1.1 above to learn how.
  2. Name your activity by typing over "Untitled Speak the Words Set"
  3. In the "Describe Task" box, write your question or prompt. 
    Ex. "What is Bart Simpson's father's name?"
  4. You can add one or more correct answers. To add more than one,  just click "Add Item". 
    Ex. Homer Simpson
    Ex. Homer Jay Simpson
    Ex. Homer
  5. To create a new prompt, click the blue "Add Question" button.
  6. Click on the check mark icon above your activity to exit
